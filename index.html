    Fiscal policy staying the course in troubled times
    The war in Ukraine has clouded the near-term economic outlook.     Our behaviours are highly influenced by social pressure.     Bitcoin is often sold as protection against adverse macroeconomic outcomes. This column argues that this depiction as a macro hedge does not stand up to scrutiny.
